Our Story

A Family Tradition

Since 1985, Annie's Pizza has been serving authentic Italian recipes passed down through three generations. Our dough is made fresh daily, our sauce simmers for hours, and every pizza is crafted with love.

What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood staple, but our commitment to quality and tradition has never wavered.
